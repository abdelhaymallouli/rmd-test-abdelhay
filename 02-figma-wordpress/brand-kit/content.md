# Atlas Coffee Tangier — Sample Copy

You are free to use, edit, or shorten the copy below. Do not invent a different brand voice.

Warm, grounded, confident. The voice of a small coffee shop that takes its beans seriously without being pretentious about it.

**Feature 2**

## Vocabulary you may use

- "Specialty coffee" — the brand is specifically a specialty coffee shop, not a generic cafe
- "Single-origin", "small batch", "slow roast" — these are real terms that fit the brand
- "Tangier" — never "Tanger" in English copy
- "The medina" — Tangier's old town, the brand is located there

## Vocabulary to avoid

- "Premium", "luxury", "world-class" — generic and untrue
- "Best coffee in the world" — overclaiming
- Excessive emojis (one or two is fine, twenty is not)
