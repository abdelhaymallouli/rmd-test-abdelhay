# Atlas Coffee Tangier — Color Palette

The brand uses **3 colors only**. Do not invent additional ones for the test.

| Role        | Name               | Hex       | When to use                                      |
| ----------- | ------------------ | --------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Primary     | Tangier Terracotta | `#B5482E` | Logo, primary CTAs, accent shapes, key headings  |
| Secondary   | Atlas Cream        | `#F5EFE3` | Page background, soft sections, card backgrounds |
| Dark / text | Espresso Charcoal  | `#1F1B16` | Body text, headings on cream, dark sections      |

## Combinations that work

* **Light section:** Cream background + Charcoal text + Terracotta CTA.
* **Dark section:** Charcoal background + Cream text + Terracotta CTA (use sparingly — once on the page maximum).
* **Hero:** Cream background, Charcoal headline, Terracotta accent (a thin line, an underline, or the CTA).

## Combinations that do NOT work — do not use

* White background with terracotta body text (low contrast).
* Pure black (`#000000`) — use Espresso Charcoal `#1F1B16` instead.
* Any shade of green, blue, or pink — they are not part of this brand.

## Accessibility check

* Charcoal `#1F1B16` on Cream `#F5EFE3` -> high contrast ratio. Safe.
* Cream `#F5EFE3` on Terracotta `#B5482E` -> \~4.5:1, just passes for body text. Prefer it for CTAs and large headings, not small body copy.
